This website helps you find Cadw sites within a few hours travel time from railway stations in Wales.

Travel times are approximate, based on travelling on the morning of Sat 4th June 2022, and include for waiting for infrequent services.

Not affliated with Cadw, Transport for Wales or Traveline Cymru. Uses transport data from multiple sources and Cadw's list of sites

Always check transport and destination details before travelling!
